About Me

I am passionate about technology, helping people and learning from different people, cultures, languages and ideas.

Roof of the world

I was born in Lithang, a remote town in Tibet. Lithang is 4,014 metres high, making it one of the highest places in the world. It has many grasslands and snow-capped mountains. The outside events had forced me to flee into exile when I was seven years old. Since then, I began my journey of discovery to different parts of the world.

A Place of Welcome

I am a junior at Luther College. My college is located in the vibrant town of Decorah in northeastern Iowa, Luther is an undergraduate liberal arts college affiliated with the Lutheran Church (ELCA).

Luther values its heritage. It was founded in 1861 by Norwegian immigrants -just as it values the diversity of its students, staff, and faculty, who hail from many different faith backgrounds and heritages.
